Blob Feeder — A Tamagotchi-style micro-game where you keep your blob alive by collecting food!
  • Guide the blob with your mouse/touch to collect shapes
  • Each level = one musical scale (do-re-mi), longer scales as you progress
  • Pick up items quickly for speed bonuses (up to 2x points)
  • Don't let your blob go hungry - it will shrink and lose points!
  • Watch the blob's face - it shows mood based on hunger and energy
