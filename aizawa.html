Aizawa Attractor
  • dx/dt = (z - b)x - dy
  • dy/dt = dx + (z - b)y
  • dz/dt = c + az - z³/3 - (x² + y²)(1 + ez) + fzx³
  • a=0.95, b=0.7, c=0.6, d=3.5, e=0.25, f=0.1
  • Cyan = slow, Magenta = fast
  • Drag to rotate
  • Scroll/pinch to zoom
  • Double-click to reset zoom
  • Press R to restart
