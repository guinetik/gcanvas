Lorenz Racer — ride the attractor in 3D
  • It forms for ~10s, then camera locks in + spawns the ship
  • Steer: A/D or ←/→
  • Throttle / Brake: W/S or ↑/↓
  • Boost (drains energy): SPACE
  • Reset: R
  • If you drift too far from the line, you detach and fall
  • During formation: drag to rotate, scroll/pinch to zoom
