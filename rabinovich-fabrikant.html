Rabinovich-Fabrikant Attractor (1979)
  • dx/dt = y(z - 1 + x²) + γx
  • dy/dt = x(3z + 1 - x²) + γy
  • dz/dt = -2z(α + xy)
  • α = 0.14, γ = 0.10
